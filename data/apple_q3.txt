Apple Inc. Q3 2023 Earnings Summary:
Apple announced financial results for its fiscal 2023 third quarter ended July 1, 2023. 
The Company posted quarterly revenue of $81.8 billion, down 1 percent year over year, and quarterly earnings per diluted share of $1.26, up 5 percent year over year. 
Services revenue reached a new all-time high of $21.2 billion, driven by over 1 billion paid subscriptions.
iPhone revenue was $39.7 billion, down 2 percent year over year, but saw strong growth in emerging markets.
Operating cash flow was a very strong $26 billion, allowing the company to return over $24 billion to shareholders during the quarter.
